Grant Award from Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISF)

We are proud to announce that Sustainogram has been selected as a recipient of a grant under the prestigious Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISF). This recognition by the Government of India validates our innovative approach to carbon credit trading and strengthens our commitment to making a positive impact.

Government Recognition

We are honored to be recognized by esteemed government institutions like the Bnest Incubation Center and Bhopal Smart City. Their support provides valuable resources and guidance, propelling us forward in our mission.

Funding of Rs 20 Lakhs (USD ~$26,000)

The grant funding of Rs 20 Lakhs (approximately USD $26,000) will be instrumental in accelerating our development and propelling us towards achieving our sustainability goals.

Valuation of Rs 18.87 Cr (USD ~$2.4 Million)

We are humbled by the valuation of Rs 18.87 Crore (approximately USD $2.4 Million) placed on Sustainogram. This signifies the immense potential investors see in our vision and motivates us to deliver exceptional results.

A Heartfelt Thank You

We extend our sincere gratitude to the Government of India, Bnest Incubation Center, Bhopal Smart City, and all our supporters for believing in Sustainogram. Your confidence fuels our passion and empowers us to create a more sustainable future for all.

This is just the beginning! We are steadfast in our commitment to revolutionize the carbon credit market with our blockchain-based platform. Stay tuned for exciting updates as we continue to break new ground in sustainability.

Together, let's build a greener tomorrow!
