Scaling Impact through Carbon Markets

A global marketplace and exchange, At Sustainogram Marketplace, we enable you to take climate action with confidence

Sustainogram is a leading Indian startup dedicated to tackling climate change through the power of the carbon credit market. Registered under DPIIT (Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade) At present, we offer credits from nature-based projects as they bring co-benefits to communities and biodiversity, in addition to providing meaningful scale, as estimates suggest they can support up to 30% of required climate change mitigation for a Below-2Â°C pathway by 2030 (Natural Climate Solutions Alliance).

All our credits are verified by global standards such as Verra and Gold Standard. We also conduct checks to ensure the credits listed have robust carbon sequestration performance, contribute meaningfully to biodiversity, and ensure social integrity, project governance & risk.

Our featured offerings.

  • Renewable Energy Certificates
  • Afforestation Credits
  • Concrete Mineralisation Credits
  • Forest Protection and Enhancement
  • Blue Carbon Credits
