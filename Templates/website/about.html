Building a Sustainable Future, Together

At Sustainogram, we are more than just a company – we're a passionate community driven by a shared mission: combating climate change and empowering a sustainable future.

We build resilient platforms, collaborate with innovative partners and foster ecosystems that help companies take practical climate mitigation action through trusted carbon credits.

Registered under DPIIT (Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade), we're a young Indian startup committed to making a global impact.

Our Story

Our story began with a simple observation, The carbon credit market, a powerful tool for emission reduction, lacked accessibility and transparency. This realization fueled
our desire to create a more effective and equitable system.

Our Approach

We believe in a two-pronged approach to sustainability.

Empowering Individuals and Businesses

We developed a user-friendly carbon calculator – a comprehensive tool that helps both individuals and businesses track, monitor, and reduce their carbon footprint. Our services encompass various sectors, including aviation, afforestation, renewable energy, and more.

Revolutionizing Carbon Trading

We are on a mission to transform the carbon credit market through the power of blockchain technology. Our upcoming blockchain-based carbon exchange promises increased transparency, liquidity, accessibility, and efficiency in carbon credit trading. This innovative platform will benefit all stakeholders, from project developers to businesses and individual investors.

Our Vision

Our vision is to lead the transformation towards a carbon-neutral future, where businesses seamlessly integrate high-quality carbon credits from diverse nature and technology-based projects. We aim to support global climate goals by promoting solutions that deliver significant environmental and community benefits, striving to achieve a Below-2°C pathway by 2030.
