Our Services

we are a passionate community of sustainability enthusiasts working towards a shared goal, reducing carbon emissions and building a greener future.

PRODUCER

The project sponsor, develops,finances, constructs, and commences commercialoperations of the project

CONSULTANT

Conceives, structures, and designs acarbon offset project and submits thedesign to a carbon offset registry

AUDITOR

The project design is auditedby a third-party verifier to confirmcompliance with registry requirements.Submits report to Registry

RETIREMENT

An end consumer purchases and“retires” the carbon offset, and theretirement is then noted on the relevantregistry

TRADE

The project sponsor / owner sells the carbonoffset into the market. Such a sale can oftenhappen through a wholesaler, but the carbonoffset remains listed on the relevant registry,which continues to track its ownership

CERTIFICATION

Registry Organisation/ Platformreviews the project document,structure, and validation report andreleased the Impact Statement i.e.Certification – Carbon Offset
